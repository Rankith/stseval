The Coach has access to three features:

  • Start List
    • yellow: gymnast stands at the event. Prepare to begin.
    • green: gymnast begins exercise.
    • red: gymnast has completed exercise.
  • Inquiry () sends a text message to the Meet Administrator:
    • scratches
    • changes to competition order
    • inquiries
    • etc.
  • Scoreboard, access to completed exercises including video.

For falls and dismounts, Coach is notified credit / no credit.

There is a fall timer:

  • First 30 seconds black.
  • Second 30 seconds red.
