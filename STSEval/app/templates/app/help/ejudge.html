For the Deduction Controller, landscape mode is recommended.

Once the exercise has begun, click the appropriate deductions.

Once the exercise is completed, review your deductions.

  • To change a deduction, click on the dot you wish to change and select the desired change.
  • Every change that is made will be outlined in white.

If you are satisfied, press Submit. The controller will reset, so be ready to start your next exercise.

Note: your deductions will be auto-submitted 20 seconds after the D-Judge releases your controller.
