When the gymnast is present, start the video recording with Start Judging.

  • You will script the exercise on paper.

When the gymnast finishes his routine, stop recording with Exercise Done.

  • This will give the E-Judges 20 seconds to review their dots.
  • After 20 seconds, all E-Jury dots are auto-submitted.

Once the deductions appear, do a quick review of the E-Jury dots.

For NCAA four judge panels, send the E-Judges to the other D-Judge with Release E-Jury.
