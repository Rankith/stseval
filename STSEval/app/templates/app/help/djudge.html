When the gymnast is present, Start Judging.



  • This starts recording.
  • Unlocks E-Jury controllers.
  • You will script the exercise on paper.

When the gymnast finishes, stop recording with Exercise Done.



  • E-Judges have 20 seconds to review their dots.
  • After 20 seconds, all E-Jury dots are auto-submitted.
  • As D-Judge, you can then edit the dots.

Once the deductions appear, do a quick review of the E-Jury dots.

  • To change a deduction, click on the dot or blank area you wish to change. Select the desired change.
  • Changes are outlined in white.

Note: for NCAA 4-judge panels, send the E-Judges to the other D-Judge with Release E-Jury.
