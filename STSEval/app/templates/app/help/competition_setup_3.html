To add a team, click Add and fill out the team information for all teams competing in the competition:

  • Instructions for the competition will be sent to the email addresses provided.
  • Passwords are autogenerated but can be changed.

To add an athlete, use import to upload an excel/csv document or click Add and fill out the athlete information individually:

  • Only teams previously added can be selected from the dropdown menu.
  • Rotations follow Olympic order (A: Floor Exercise, B: Pommel Horse, etc.)
  • Unselect the appropriate checkbox(es) for event(s) an athlete is not competing.
  • Unselect the appropriate checkbox(es) for event(s) not counted for the team score.

Once athletes are entered, you can drag their names to re-order them on the event.
